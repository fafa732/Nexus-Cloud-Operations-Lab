# Day 14 — Jira Service Management Setup

## Purpose

This folder documents the Jira Service Management setup for the Nexus Cloud Operations Lab.

The goal was to create a realistic service desk platform for documenting support tickets, access requests, incidents, and operational tasks.

## Scenario

Nexus Retail Group needed a structured ticketing platform for MSP-style support work.

Jira Service Management was selected as the official service desk tool for the project from Day 14 onward.

## Work Completed

- Created the Nexus Cloud Operations Service Desk
- Used Jira Service Management for support ticket tracking
- Created initial support tickets
- Organised tickets by status
- Reviewed priorities and ticket workflow
- Captured service desk evidence for the portfolio
