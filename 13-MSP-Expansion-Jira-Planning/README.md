# Day 13 — MSP Expansion and Jira Service Management Planning

## Purpose

This folder documents the MSP expansion planning phase of the Nexus Cloud Operations Lab.

The goal was to move the project from a single cloud administration lab into a more realistic service desk and MSP-style support structure.

## Scenario

Nexus Retail Group needed support processes that looked closer to a real IT support or MSP environment.

This included planning ticket categories, priorities, escalation paths, SLA thinking, and a more formal service desk workflow.

## Work Completed

- Reviewed the need for MSP-style ticketing
- Planned support ticket categories
- Planned escalation workflow
- Planned SLA and priority mapping
- Selected Jira Service Management as the official service desk platform
- Prepared the structure for the Jira setup completed on Day 14

## MSP Support Areas Planned

The support structure included:

- Access requests
- Identity incidents
- Offboarding reviews
- RBAC validation
- File permission requests
- Backup readiness tasks
- Monitoring and alert tickets
- Endpoint management tasks

## Skills Demonstrated

- MSP support planning
- Service desk workflow planning
- Ticket category planning
- SLA awareness
- Escalation planning
- IT support process documentation
